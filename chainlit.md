# A/B Testing Analysis Agent

Welcome to the A/B Testing Analysis Agent! I'm here to help you analyze your A/B test experiments with comprehensive statistical analysis and interactive visualizations.

## What I Can Do

- **Load and analyze CSV data** - Upload your experiment data and I'll explore its structure
- **Run statistical A/B tests** - Get t-tests, effect sizes, confidence intervals, and power analysis
- **Segment analysis** - Analyze results across different customer segments
- **Generate reports** - Comprehensive summaries with actionable recommendations
- **Create visualizations** - Interactive charts to explore your results

## Getting Started

1. Upload your CSV file using the attachment button
2. I'll detect your columns and help you map them correctly
3. Specify your treatment and control groups
4. Run your analysis!
5. Ask me to show charts or visualize the results

## Required Data Format

Your CSV should contain:
- **Group column** - Treatment/control indicator
- **Effect/metric column** - The outcome you're measuring
- **Segment column** (optional) - For segmented analysis

## Available Visualizations

- **Dashboard** - Comprehensive multi-chart overview
- **Treatment vs Control** - Compare group means
- **Effect Sizes** - With 95% confidence intervals
- **P-values** - Significance testing results
- **Power Analysis** - Statistical power by segment
- **Cohen's d** - Standardized effect sizes
- **Sample Sizes** - Group size comparison
- **Waterfall** - Effect contribution by segment
